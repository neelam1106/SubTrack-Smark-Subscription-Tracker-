About SubTrack

What is SubTrack?

SubTrack is a web-based subscription tracking system designed to help users manage all their digital subscriptions in one place. It allows users to add, track, and monitor subscription services such as Netflix, Spotify, Amazon Prime, and other paid platforms.

Why was SubTrack built?

In today’s digital world, users often subscribe to multiple services and forget their renewal dates, leading to unwanted auto-deductions. SubTrack was built to solve this problem by providing timely reminders, clear spending insights, and a centralized platform to manage all subscriptions efficiently.

Target Users

  • Students using OTT and learning platforms
  • Working professionals with multiple subscriptions
  • Frequent OTT and digital service users
  • Anyone who wants better control over digital spending

Subscription Billing Logic

SubTrack separates monthly and yearly subscriptions to provide accurate financial insights. Monthly subscriptions are counted only in the monthly total, while yearly subscriptions are calculated separately without converting monthly plans into yearly costs.

This ensures that users clearly understand their actual spending pattern without misleading calculations.

Grace Period Feature

SubTrack includes a built-in grace period feature. After a subscription reaches its end date, it remains visible in the system for an additional 3 days. This grace period allows users to review expired subscriptions before they are permanently removed.

After the grace period ends, the system automatically removes the expired subscription to keep the dashboard clean and up-to-date.

Key Features

  • Monthly and yearly expense tracking
  • Renewal date reminders
  • Grace period for expired subscriptions
  • Secure user authentication
  • Simple and user-friendly dashboard
